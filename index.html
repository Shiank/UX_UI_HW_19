Hi there! I'm Shian

I'm a UI/UX Designer with a Background in Graphic Design. Knowing the possibilities of design and what it can do society, It drives me to solve issues users have by making the digital experience more pleasing and instinctual through clever design processes and implementations.

About Me

I am an adventurous person, I loves learning new things, traveling to new places, and involving myself in all things creative. It took me a while to figure out what I wanted to do with life, I went from being that person who just graduated college in a pandemic with no idea what she's doing to a multidisciplinary designer.

Nothing give me greater satisfaction than being able to use all the skills that I acquired to create engaging and beautiful user-centered designs, and with the use of UI, it is a big factor in my growing passion for design. There is always so much more to learn and I am eager to continue expanding my knowledge further.
